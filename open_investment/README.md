# Open Finance for All: Your Money, Your Rules

Democratizing financial services and giving individuals control over their money and financial data.

## Overview

This subject explores the vision of enabling every person, regardless of their financial knowledge or technical expertise, to manage and invest their money while maintaining complete control over their assets.
